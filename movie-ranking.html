My Top 5 Movies

1. Arjun Reddy

Arjun Reddy is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga. The film stars Vijay Deverakonda and Shalini Pandey in lead roles. It tells the story of a high-functioning alcoholic surgeon who spirals into self-destruction after his girlfriend is forced to marry someone else.

Salaar: Part 1 - The CeaseFire is an Indian action thriller film directed by Prashanth Neel. The film stars Prabhas in the lead role. It is expected to be a high-octane action film with a gripping storyline.

4. Tamasha

Tamasha is a 2015 Indian romantic drama film directed by Imtiaz Ali. The film stars Ranbir Kapoor and Deepika Padukone in lead roles. It explores the themes of self-discovery and the societal pressures that shape our lives.

5. Good Will Hunting

Good Will Hunting is a 1997 American drama film directed by Gus Van Sant. The film stars Matt Damon and Robin Williams in lead roles. It tells the story of a young janitor at MIT who has a genius-level intellect but struggles with his past and his potential.
